Cobb Salad :

This Cobb Salad Recipe will blow your mind. The textures are interesting
and varied, the flavors are fresh and bright, and the dressing...wait until you try this dressing!

Ingredients :

Meats

  • 2 pieces Bacon, cooked
  • 1 Chicken breast, cooked
  • 1 Romaine heart

Fruits & Vegetables

  • 1/2 Avocado
  • 6 Cherry tomatoes
  • 1 Red onion

Dressing

  • 1/2 tbsp Honey dijon mustard
  • 1 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 tbsp White wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper

Cheese

  • 1/4 cup Feta
